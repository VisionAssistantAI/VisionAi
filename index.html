Voice Commands to Control Vision

With Vision, you can easily control your computer using simple voice commands:

  • Activate Vision: Say "VISION ON"
  • Deactivate Vision: Say "VISION OVERRIDE"
  • Shut down Vision: Say "VISION SHUTDOWN"
  • Control the Mouse with Your Face: Say "VISION EYES ON"
  • Open a Website: Say "OPEN" followed by the website name, e.g., "OPEN YouTube"
  • Run Applications: Say "RUN" followed by the application name, e.g., "RUN Outlook"
  • Create New Folders: Say "CREATE FOLDER" followed by the folder name, e.g., "CREATE FOLDER Vision"
  • Control Windows: Say "MAXIMIZE" to maximize a window or "MINIMIZE" to minimize a window
  • Search: Say "SEARCH FOR" followed by a search term, e.g., "SEARCH FOR Vision AI"
  • Scroll: Say "PAGE UP" or "PAGE DOWN" to scroll the current page
  • Control Volume: Say "VOLUME UP" or "VOLUME DOWN"

Control Your Computer with Your Face

Vision enables you to use your face to control your computer mouse:

  • Ensure your camera is on and working.
  • Position your face directly in front of the camera. This works best with a single display.
  • To click, simply blink a little slower. Upon eye opening, Vision will click where the mouse is positioned.
